Untitled poem 1

wooooorting
sloged bavary: a coined tremble
transporting hosiery
a good bevel
aborting honesty
the food rubble
assorted beans in my palm
upturned (palm not beans)
rains tiny rocks for the cement cracks
question all- freak out when stampeding hobgoblins yelp, "alas!"
